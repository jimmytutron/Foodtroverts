About Foodtroverts

Ever been hungry but wanted some company, or just some good food and conversation? Are all of your friends busy during your lunch hours? Maybe there is a new place you want to try in your area but your tastebuds are more adventurous than the people you would usually eat out with? Good news… We’re here to help!

Foodtroverts is a platform built with the intention of bringing people together, everyone has to eat so why not add some company? Often times people associate food with dating - so we are here to get rid of that stigma. However - new friends, great experiences, networking, and maybe a special connection... it’s ALL welcomed here after a good meal!

What do you have to lose? Get started today!

How To

You’ve made it this far, so let us do the rest of the work for you! Simply navigate to the ‘Get Started’ form and input your name, location desired, preference of time, as well as the type of cuisine you’re feeling curious about.

After submitting this information, we will then take the reigns for you and find your match - just like that! Soon after someone in your area has the same interests as you, you will be notified upon submission with a time and the exact restaurant you will be expected at as well as your new buddies name.

Have fun! Wishing you many more - new friends and happy meals! -Foodtroverts, your fellow foodies.
